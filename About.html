Kalki

Main Points:

  • Based on the concept of Kalki Avatar, the final incarnation of Lord Vishnu in Hindu mythology.
  • Explores themes of good vs. evil, the end of Kaliyuga, and the rise of a new age.
  • May include elements of prophecy, war, spirituality, and social issues.

Why Read It?

  • If you enjoy mythology and apocalyptic themes, this book will be engaging.
  • Provides philosophical and moral insights related to modern-day problems.
  • A mix of history, mythology, and fiction, making it a compelling read.
